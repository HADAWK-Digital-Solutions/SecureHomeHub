Scans Page

Introduction

The Scans feature of the Secure Home Hub allows you to perform various types of scans to ensure the security and health of your connected IoT devices. This section provides details on different scan options, their scheduling, and how to initiate scans effectively.

Types of Scans

  • Manual Scans: Scans initiated manually by the user.
  • Nightly Scans: Automated scans scheduled to run nightly.
  • Post-Alert Scans: Scans triggered automatically after specific security alerts.

Viewing Scan Results

To view the results of the scans performed by the Secure Home Hub, navigate to the Scans page from the main menu. The scan results will provide information about the scanned devices, identified vulnerabilities, and overall network health.

Managing Scans

  • Scan Times: Access detailed information about the times manual scans were initiated. This section provides a chronological list of manual scan instances along with their start times.
  • Scan after Alerts: Configure automatic scans triggered after specific security alerts. This ensures a proactive approach to security following identified threats.
  • Quick Enable Nightly Scans: Enable or disable the automated nightly scans for convenience. Nightly scans help identify and address potential issues on a regular basis.
  • Scan Script: Execute a customized scan script to perform advanced scans based on specific requirements. This allows users to tailor scans to their network's unique needs.

Help and Support

If you encounter issues or have questions related to scans, refer to the help documentation provided with the Secure Home Hub. Additionally, you can contact our support team for assistance.
