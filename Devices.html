Devices Page

Introduction

The Devices feature of the Secure Home Hub allows you to manage and monitor your connected IoT devices. This section provides details on network topology, device connections, intrusion detection on devices, and viewing device ports.

Network Topology

  • Topology: Explore the network topology to visualize the layout of connected devices. This includes information on how devices are interconnected within your home network.

Connecting Devices

  • Connect Devices: Easily add new devices to the Secure Home Hub by following the step-by-step connection process. This includes scanning for devices, selecting the device type, and establishing a secure connection.

Intrusion Detection on Devices

  • Scan for Intrusion on Devices: Initiate a scan specifically focused on detecting any intrusion attempts or suspicious activities on your connected IoT devices.

Displaying Ports

  • Display Ports: View the open ports on your connected devices. This provides insights into the network services running on each device and helps identify potential security vulnerabilities.

Help and Support

If you encounter issues or have questions related to device management, refer to the help documentation provided with the Secure Home Hub. Additionally, you can contact our support team for assistance.
