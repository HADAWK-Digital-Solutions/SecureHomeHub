Alerts and Notifications

Introduction

The Alerts feature of the Secure Home Hub provides real-time notifications and warnings regarding the security and status of your connected IoT devices. This section outlines the different types of alerts, how they are triggered, and steps you can take to manage and respond to alerts effectively.

Types of Alerts

The Secure Home Hub can generate various types of alerts based on the security events detected. Here are some common alert categories:

  • Intrusion Detection: Alerts triggered when unauthorized access or suspicious activities are detected.
  • Device Connectivity: Alerts related to the status and connectivity of your IoT devices.
  • System Notifications: General system messages and notifications.

Viewing Alerts

To view the alerts generated by the Secure Home Hub, navigate to the Alerts page from the main menu. The list of alerts will display relevant information, including the date and time of the alert, and a brief description of the event.

Managing Alerts

Alerts can be managed and reviewed through the Alerts page. You can perform the following actions:

  • Alert Times: Access detailed information about the times alerts were triggered. This section provides a chronological list of all alerts along with the date and time of each occurrence.
  • Alert Triggers: Explore the specific triggers or events that led to each alert. This includes information about the detected activity or event that triggered the alert.
  • Alert Priority: Understand the priority levels assigned to different types of alerts. Priorities may range from low to high, indicating the severity of the security event.

Intrusion Detection

The Intrusion Detection feature of the Secure Home Hub actively monitors your network for potential security threats. When an intrusion is detected, an alert is triggered. Here are the steps to manage and respond to intrusion detection alerts:

  1. Review the details of the intrusion alert to understand the nature of the potential threat.
  2. If required, take immediate actions to secure your network, such as isolating affected devices or updating security settings.
  3. For further assistance, refer to the user manual or contact customer support.

Help and Support

If you encounter issues or have questions related to alerts, refer to the help documentation provided with the Secure Home Hub. Additionally, you can contact our support team for assistance.
